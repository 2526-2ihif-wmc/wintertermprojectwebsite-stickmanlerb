Top Attractions in Oradea

Oradea offers a wide variety of attractions, from historic landmarks and elegant Art Nouveau buildings to relaxing thermal baths and beautiful walking areas.

Black Eagle Palace

One of Oradea’s most iconic Art Nouveau buildings, famous for its glass passage and architectural details.

Oradea Fortress

A medieval pentagonal fortress that played a key role in the city’s military and cultural history.

Union Square

The heart of Oradea’s historic center, surrounded by beautiful buildings, cafés, and landmarks.

Moon Church

A unique church featuring a mechanical moon that displays the phases of the moon.

Aquapark Nymphaea

A modern thermal water complex offering pools, slides, and relaxation areas.

Crișul Repede River

A peaceful riverside area perfect for walks, cycling, and enjoying the city’s atmosphere.

Walking & Relaxation

Oradea is known for its clean streets, green spaces, and pedestrian zones. The river promenade and city parks offer ideal places to relax, walk, or enjoy a coffee in the open air.
