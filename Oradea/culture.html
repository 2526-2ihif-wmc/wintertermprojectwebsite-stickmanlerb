History of Oradea

Oradea is one of the oldest cities in Romania, with a rich history shaped by medieval fortresses, Austro-Hungarian influence, and modern development.

The Oradea Fortress played a major role in the city’s strategic importance, while the Art Nouveau period transformed the city center into an architectural jewel.
